About Frozen Kadai

Farm Frozen, Chef Ready - Since 2020

Our Story

From local farms to your freezer, preserving freshness at -18°C.

Founded in 2020, Frozen Kadai began with a simple mission: to provide premium quality frozen meat without compromising on taste or nutrition. Inspired by traditional Indian meat markets (KariKadai), we combined age-old butchering expertise with modern freezing technology.

What started as a small operation in Palani has now grown to serve thousands of happy customers across the region. Our commitment to quality, hygiene, and cold chain integrity remains at the heart of everything we do.
